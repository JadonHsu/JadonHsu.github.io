Reds Hackathon 2024

Data Analytics Project using Large Data Sets

Competed in the 2024 Reds Hackathon. Collaborative group project to analyze MLB pitchers stats and define if they were better suited for a different role than they were currently in. Created a model using SIERA (Skill-Interactive Earned Run Average) and identified several stats which were more important for releif pitchers than starts and vice versa. Using this model, we ran predicted current starters who would be better suited as relievers and examined their seasons which the model showed that they were better suited in a different role. Examining their current team situation, contract situation, age, and other factors not included in the emperical model, we identified 3 targest to change to a different role in the upcoming 2024 season: Marco Gonzales, Tyler Anderson, and Kyle Hendricks.
